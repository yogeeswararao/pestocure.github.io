We use drill fill seal (DFS) Treatment For Control Termites With Bayer Household Ouderless Insecticides. DFS Means Drill, Fill, and Seal it will be carried out in 3 different stages they are, Stage 1: Drilling above wall skirting with feet gap of 10mm size inclined holes with 4 to 6 mm depth with hammer drill. Stage 2: Filling insecticides through a pressure pump and injecting insecticide around wooden frames like doors, windows, cupboards etc... Within the holes provided near frames or choukats. Spraying Bayer Agenda with Turpent oil on doors, windows, cupboards doors, etc... For surface protection... Stage 3: Sealing all the filled holes with white cement to regain the strength and finish of the wall.

By carrying all these 3 stages your flat will be protected from Termites for up to 3 years
